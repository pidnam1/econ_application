In this experiment, you will act as both a test-taker and a helper.

In some rounds, you will act as a helper by helping a test-taker.

In other rounds, the computer will act as a helper.

The role of the test-taker
  • Answer all the questions on the test.
Your role as the helper
  • When they ask, you will supply them with hints.
  • You will only use the hints we provide to you. This means that the quality of hints is not determined by you.
  • You will only interact through the tablet.
  • You are not allowed to talk to them.
  • You are not allowed to create your own hints or give them any other information.
  • However, you may decide how many hints in total to give them.
  • You will be able to see if they take a hint.
