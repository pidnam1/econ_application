In this experiment, you will act as both a test-taker and a helper.

In some rounds, you will act as a test-taker and a helper will help you.

In other rounds, you will act as a test-taker and a computer will help you.

Your role as the test-taker
  • Answer all the questions on the test.
The role of the helper
  • When you ask, your helper will supply you with hints.
  • They will only use the hints we provide to them. This means that the quality of hints is not determined by them.
  • You will interact through the tablet only.
  • The helper is not allowed to talk to you.
  • The helper is not allowed to create their own hints or give you any other information.
  • However, they may decide how many hints in total to give you.
  • Helper will be able to see if you take a hint.
